How to play

Who Should Play?

Escapium is perfect for everyone. There are no special skills needed to have a great time. Bring your friends, family, co-workers, just a bunch of random people and get fun.

For Team Building

Our escape rooms are designed for intense participation from the group trying to escape. It's perfect for team building experience in your city.

For Special Events

Whatever the celebration Escapium is here to take it to another level. Book your room and contact us to see how we can help make anv special occasion even better.

What is an Escape Room?

An escape game is a real-life experience where participants are locked in a room and must use elements of the room, the group's collective knowledge, and wits to discover clues, solve puzzles, and escape and/or find the missing item within a set amount of time.
